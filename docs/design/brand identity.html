Corporate identity & logo

The EPFL logo contributes to the recognition and image of the institution: it must include ALL the letters of the acronym and full name, placed beneath it. Those two elements are inseparable.

Rules and recommendations

Exclusivity

To the exception of external partnership units or projects, the EPFL logo is the only official logo within the institution. No other logos or individual units within EPFL can be added.

Use of EPFL logo

The EPFL logo can be used on:

  • All EPFL websites and pages (URL must include epfl.ch)
  • All visual communication material (social media channels, slides, films, etc.)
  • All communication systems (newsletters, email signatures, etc.)

This rule is valid for all above-mentioned materials published by EPFL offical units and associations, as well as their partners in joint activities (eg. forums, conferences, seminars, lectures, academic or cultural events, etc.).

Logo placement

The EPFL logo must be placed at the top-left corner.

Use by students associations

The use of EPFL logo by students associations is subject to certain terms and conditions. Please read about them on the DAF associations website.

Use of capital and lowercase

Ecole polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne
Only the word "Ecole" takes a capital letter. In English, we refer simply to "EPFL", which is one of two Swiss Federal Institutes of Technology.
